Gravitational N-Body Simulation

How to use

Controls

  • Pan - Right Click
  • Zoom - Scroll or Middle Click
  • Fullscreen mode - F11

Options

  • Mass - the mass of the next created body
  • Density - the density of the next created body
  • G Constant - the gravitational Constant
  • Time Per Step - how much time passes in Simulation beetween steps: faster simulation, does not affect fps, but less precise
  • Steps Per Frame - how many steps are calculated for each frame, slower fps, does not affect precission

Presets

  • Empty Universe - just an empty, boring Universe
  • A Single Star - one star in the middle of the screen
  • Solar System - a solar system formed by a sun with huge mass and some planets with almost negligible mass
  • Binary Stars - two stars of equal mass orbiting their center of mass
  • Triple Stars - a triple star system
  • Planet w/ Satellite - a planet which has a natural satellite
  • Protoplanetary - protoplanetary disk orbiting a star

References and acknowledgements

  • Rendered using THREE.js
  • Random colors generated using randomColor
